Cookies and opt-out

When you visit this website, we leave a small text file (a "cookie") on your computer. This helps us measure how many visits we've had and which pages are most viewed.

This website does not show a consent banner for cookies, why?

Our tracking service, Piwik is configured in compliance with the "Cookies" recommandations (French) from the French authorities (CNIL) (i.e. Piwik anonymizes your IP address to make it impossible to link your visit to you personally).

I contribute to your data, can I access it?

Of course! The analytics data for OpenFisca is freely available on stats.data.gouv.fr.
